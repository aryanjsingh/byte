CERT Resilience Management Model (CERT-RMM)

1. Asset Definition and Management (ADM):
The purpose of Asset Definition and Management is to identify, document, and manage organizational assets during their lifecycle to ensure that they support the organization's mission and sustain value.

2. Access Management (AM):
The purpose of Access Management is to ensure that access granted to assets is commensurate with their business value and the organization's risk appetite.

3. Incident Management and Control (IMC):
The purpose of Incident Management and Control is to establish processes to detect, analyze, and respond to incidents designated by the organization as requiring specific handling.

4. Vulnerability Analysis and Resolution (VAR):
The purpose of Vulnerability Analysis and Resolution is to identify, analyze, and manage vulnerabilities in organizational assets.

Key Practice: Establish a strong password policy (e.g., minimum 14 characters, complexity requirements, rotation every 90 days if without MFA).
Key Practice: Implement Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA) for all remote access and administrative accounts.
